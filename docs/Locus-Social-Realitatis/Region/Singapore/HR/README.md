# HR

- How the public personal it's structure in Singapore?

| **Subsystem**              | **Description**                                        | **HR Governance**                            | **Autonomy Level**            | **Typical Functions**                                           |
| -------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Civil Service**          | Core administrative personnel inside Ministries        | PSC (senior), PSD (policy), Personnel Boards | Low autonomy (centralized)    | Policy, strategy, governance, regulation                        |
| **Statutory Boards**       | Operational and technical agencies delivering services | Individual Boards + PSD guidance             | High autonomy (decentralized) | Execution, engineering, implementation, services                |
| **Public Service (Whole)** | Entire government workforce                            | PSD (strategic HR), CSC (training)           | Mixed                         | Whole-of-government culture, capability, digital transformation |
