## Research Systems

| **System Type**                                 | **Acronym / Program**                       | **Core Purpose**                                                                | **Main Actors**                          | **Notable Examples**                                   |
| ----------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| 🧪 **Cooperative Research Centers**             | **IUCRC** (USA)                             | Pre-competitive R\&D co-funded by industry & NSF                                | Industry, University, NSF                | IUCRC (NSF – USA)                                      |
| 🧭 **Engineering Research Centers**             | **ERC** (USA)                               | Large-scale interdisciplinary research solving grand challenges                 | Academia, Industry, Government           | NSF ERC Program (USA)                                  |
| 🏢 **University Research Consortia**            | –                                           | Joint academic centers with multiple corporate/government sponsors              | Universities, Industry                   | Semiconductor Research Corp., IMEC                     |
| 🔁 **Translational Research Accelerators**      | **I-Corps**, **POC**, **Bridge Funding**    | Moving research toward commercialization via mentorship & funding               | Researchers, NSF/NIH/DOE, Entrepreneurs  | NSF I-Corps, NIH REACH                                 |
| 🧰 **Technology Innovation Partnerships**       | **TIP**, **ATP** (historical)               | Support industry-led tech development with public-private co-funding            | Government, Industry                     | NIST TIP (now phased out), ARPA-like programs          |
| 🏛️ **National Research Networks**              | –                                           | Federated, domain-specific centers with thematic focus                          | Funders, Universities, Labs              | EPSRC Networks (UK), NCCR (Switzerland), CRCs (AU)     |
| ⚙️ **Mission-Oriented Research Programs**       | **ARPA**, **DARPA**, **ARPA-E**, **HSARPA** | High-risk, high-impact R\&D with centralized project management                 | Program managers, Contractors, Labs      | DARPA (DoD), ARPA-E (DOE)                              |
| 🔬 **Applied Research Institutes**              | –                                           | Large-scale applied R\&D with industry/public sector needs                      | Government-funded research organizations | Fraunhofer (Germany), TNO (Netherlands), VTT (Finland) |
| 🎓 **Graduate Innovation Training Programs**    | **IGERT**, **GRFP-RA**, **CDT**             | Training PhD students in interdisciplinary and innovation-oriented environments | NSF, Universities                        | NSF IGERT (USA), UK CDT Programs                       |
| 🧠 **Knowledge Transfer and Commercialization** | **KTP**, **TTO**, **SBIR/STTR**             | Translate academic R\&D to industry/startups via funding and partnerships       | Universities, SMEs, Government           | SBIR/STTR (USA), KTP (UK), TTOs worldwide              |
