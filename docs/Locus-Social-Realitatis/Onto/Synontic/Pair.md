## Case Study

> A match is a relationship or pairing between actors.

| Domain       | Actors                  | Match                   |
| ------------ | ----------------------- | ----------------------- |
| Labor market | Workers – Firms         | Employment relationship |
| Education    | Students – Universities | Admission placement     |
| Finance      | Borrowers – Lenders     | Credit relationship     |
| Production   | Firms – Suppliers       | Supply contract         |
| Social       | Individuals             | Marriage                |
