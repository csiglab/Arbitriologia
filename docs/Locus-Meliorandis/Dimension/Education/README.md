# Educational Field

> **Education**: A formal or informal process of acquiring knowledge, cognitive skills, and cultural understanding to enable personal and societal development.

> **Training**: A structured process of developing specific practical skills or competencies to perform particular tasks or jobs effectively.

## Formulation

- Technical Training Ecosystem (TTE): Development of practical and technical competencies.
- Science & Engineering Preparation Ecosystem (SEPE): Acquisition of foundational scientific and engineering knowledge.
- General Formation Ecosystem (GFE): Development of cognitive, cultural, social, and ethical competencies for broad personal and societal formation.
