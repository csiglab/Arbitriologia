## Guiding Questions

* What is the **ontological status** of the term *industry*?

    * Is *industry* primarily an **ontic category** (a real, materially instantiated subsystem of production)?
    * An **epistemic construct** (a classificatory lens used for analysis, measurement, or policy)?
    * Or a **strategic construct** (a purposive aggregation used in planning, coordination, or intervention)?

* Under what conditions does *industry* shift between **ontic**, **epistemic**, and **strategic** roles?

* What are the **constitutive elements** of an industry (firms, technologies, skills, supply chains, institutions, standards)?

* At what **level of analysis** does the concept of industry operate (firm-level aggregation, sectoral system, national productive structure, global value chain)?

* How does the definition of an industry vary across **analytical contexts** (economic theory, industrial policy, national accounts, corporate strategy)?

* What **boundaries** delineate one industry from another, and how stable are these boundaries over time?

* How do **technological change**, **organizational recomposition**, and **institutional evolution** alter the internal coherence of an industry?

* What is the history of the **term**?

* What is the relation with the term **Industry**?
