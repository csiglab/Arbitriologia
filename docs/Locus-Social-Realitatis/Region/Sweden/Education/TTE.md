## Structure

> Which is the Institutional Typology in the Ecosystem?

| Paradigm                             | Institution                                              | Description                                                       | Role                                                                                                            | Instance(s)                                                                     |
| ------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Foundational Education               | Municipal Schools (Grundskola & Gymnasium)               | Compulsory and upper-secondary education with vocational tracks   | Provide basic technical skills, career orientation, and preparatory training for vocational or higher education | Local municipal gymnasier offering yrkesprogram                                 |
| Vocational & Technical Training      | Yrkeshögskolor / Komvux                                  | Short-cycle, industry-aligned vocational programs for adults      | Develop job-ready technical skills and reskilling/upskilling opportunities                                      | Regional technical colleges, municipal adult education programs                 |
| Higher Education                     | Universities & University Colleges                       | Advanced technical education and applied sciences programs        | Deliver theoretical foundations, research, and advanced professional skills                                     | KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Chalmers University, Lund University         |
| Industry-Affiliated Training         | Corporate Training Centers                               | Internal corporate programs for specific technical skills         | Upskill employees, provide apprenticeships, maintain sector-specific expertise                                  | Ericsson Training Center, Volvo Group Academy                                   |
| Industry-Affiliated Training         | Sectoral Training Associations                           | Industry or union-led training for sector-specific certifications | Align workforce skills with industry standards and certification requirements                                   | Swedish ICT Training Association, Manufacturing sector associations             |
| Public Governance & Regulation       | Skolverket, Yrkeshögskolemyndigheten, Arbetsförmedlingen | Government agencies overseeing education and employment programs  | Define curricula, fund vocational programs, align training with labor market needs                              | National Agency for Education (Skolverket), Swedish Higher Vocational Authority |
| Private & Non-Governmental Providers | Private Technical Schools / NGOs                         | Flexible, niche, or inclusion-focused technical training          | Complement public education with specialized, short-cycle, or socially inclusive programs                       | Coding bootcamps, non-profit technical reskilling initiatives                   |


## Policy Body(s)

- Swedish National Agency for Education (Skolverket)
- Swedish Higher Vocational Education Authority (Yrkeshögskolemyndigheten)
- Arbetsförmedlingen (Public Employment Service)
