# Matching Theory

> Matching Theory studies how actors in a system are paired, allocated, or associated with one another under constraints and preferences. The goal is to understand how stable interaction structures (matches) emerge from decentralized choices.

> A match is a pairing or association between actors within a system that establishes a potential or actual interaction relationship.
